About our team


Welcome to the Human-Centered AI group of the Computer Science Department of the Faculty of Electrical Engineering Sarajevo. Our research centers around the goal of developing artificial intelligence systems that can understand, interact and collaborate with humans in natural and ethical ways. We aim to create AI solutions that can enhance human capabilities, well-being and social good, while respecting human values, preferences and autonomy. We work on various topics such as natural language processing, computer vision, human-computer interaction, explainable AI, and AI security.
